Hello there!

Here is all there's to know about me that is not UX! A glimpse at all the important events in my life of 24 years around the sun :)

2020

  • 3rd February: Began work as a Design Lead on Frolic
  • I started working as the Design Lead on a research project in the OH! Lab under the guidance of Dr. Jessica Hammer and advisors Adela Kapuscinska and Vicky(Tai Chun) Lin. I was working on testing and iterating on an app called 'Frolic' which aims to get girls to be more active and generate new games for them.
  • 13th January: Began work on Capstone Project
  • I started working on our 8-month log capstone project for the master's program with The Pittsburgh Foundation. Our project aimed at Envisioning the Future of Responsible Philanthropy for our clients. To know more about work on this project click on this link!

2019

  • 21st August: Started my Master's degree at Carnegie Mellon University
  • I started my 12 month long intensive master's program to expand my skill set as a UX designer. Besides the core courses such as User Reaserch Methods, Interaction Design Studio, Programming Usable Interfaces I also took electives such as Persuasive Design and Designing for Conversations too help me grow as an Interaction designer.
  • 21st July: Left job at Wipro Technologies Ltd.
  • I left my job to pursue my Master's degree at CMU. From my experience at Wipro I learnt about agile methodologies working on a scrum team for an internal tool. I was resposible for developing a data validation and verification tool for internal use to help better strategize internal operations.

2018

  • 19th July: Started job at Wipro Technologies Ltd
  • I joined Wipro Technologies Ltd. as a new hire from college in the role of a Project Engineer. I was onboarded on the Market Data Support Team for the Charles Schwab account to work as a Software Developer.
  • 24th May: Graduated with my Bachelor's degree
  • Completed my bachelor's degree with 7.71/10 CGPA and a whole lot of memories!
  • 30th March: Completed a course in Print and Publishing
  • I did this course to strengthen my skills as a visual designer. I got to learn design methodologies such as colour, grid, composition, wireframing, etc. This is where I got introduced to and got hands-on experience working with design softwares such as Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and CorelDRAW.

2017

  • 10th July: Completed a nanodegree in Front-end Web Development
  • Learned HTML, CSS and JS. I used this knowledge to work on some hands-on projects during the course as well as strengthen my skills as a UX developer.

2014

  • 10th August: Became a member of Junoon Streetplay and Mad Ads Society
  • Joined a street theatre group in my college which performed plays on different social issues to create awareness and help educate the audiences. I was a part of this team all through college which participated in national and state level street theatre competitions as well representing the college. I had the opportunity of leading the team in my final year and winning over 15 competitions. From my time in the team I learnt how to lead a team, collaborate on a vision and tell a story for a compelling cause.
  • 28th July: Started my Bachelor's degree in College of Engineering Pune
  • Started my Bachelor's in engineering majoring in Information Technology at COEP. Took courses like Database Management Systems, Graphics and Multimedia, Human Computer Interaction and Intro to Psychology to name a few. My Bachelor's was the place where I first came to know about HCI and UX Design.
  • 15th April: Graduated from High School
  • Completed my schooling with a focus in Physics, Chemistry, Maths and Computer Sciences. And, decided to pursue a bachelor's in engineering after high school in Computer Sciences.

2002

  • 15th April: Started grade school at Indian School Muscat
  • Went to school here for 12 years from 1st to 12th grade. Got to learn and grow as a public speaker from middle school through high school as well as learn languages such as Hindi and Arabic (which I can still read and write and speak common phrases of!)

1996

  • Born on 4th April
  • Born in Mumbai, India. I went back to live with my mother to grow up in Muscat, Oman, a beautiful country in the Middle East near the Arabian Sea.
